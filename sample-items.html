First, let us look at the vertebrate group. A vertebrate is an animal with a backbone. This backbone is a line of hard bones down its back. Mammals and birds are in this group. They have a strong trait of having bones inside. This helps them grow tall and stand up. Dogs and horses are vertebrates.
